About Me

I am currently a second year student at The University of Waterloo, pursuing a BS in Mechanical Engineering. My studies have given me a strong background in Mechanical design and analysis which synergizes well with the projects I take on outside of class. I love to design, develop, and mock-up many different things, currently this includes a sensor monitoring system for an Aquaponics enclosure, a pneumatic-actuator powered exoskeleton hand and a personal project to reclaim broken/recycled assemblies from old ink-jet printers to build my own 3d extruder. I love facing challenging problems and being able to analyse them and design innovative solutions. My work and my projects reflect my desire to apply my engineering knowledge to the real world, and then see them through to completion.
