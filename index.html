FallSafe Personal Assistant

Fallsafe is a project I worked on for Hack The North in 2016. The device is meant for elderly in retirement homes. The device is clipped to the user's belt and an IMU detects if a fall has occured. After fall detection, an alert is sent via the PagerDuty API to alert the resident nurse that the patient may be in need. The project went on to win the PagerDuty sponsor prize.
